About UTC Time

What Is UTC Time?

UTC (Coordinated Universal Time) is the primary global time standard used to regulate clocks and timekeeping worldwide.

Why Is UTC Used Worldwide?

UTC provides a single, consistent reference time that avoids confusion caused by time zone differences.

  • Standard reference for servers, APIs, and databases
  • Used in aviation, navigation, space, and military operations
  • Prevents daylight saving time errors
  • Ensures accurate logging and scheduling

UTC vs Local Time

Local time depends on geographic location and daylight saving rules, while UTC remains the same everywhere.

Who Uses UTC Time?

UTC time is used by software systems, servers, developers, financial markets, aviation authorities, and international organizations to maintain consistent and accurate time across regions.
